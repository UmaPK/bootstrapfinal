Johnson Community strongly believes that monetary constraints should not be an obstacle for a student to have access to quality education. So, the university extends Scholarship Scheme to students on various parameters. More than 30000 students have been awarded with scholarships worth Rs.300+ crores in last 5 years.

Scholarship on the basis of LPUNEST

Johnson community conducts its own test LPUNEST (National Entrance & Scholarship Test). This test forms the basis of eligibility for some of the programmes and scholarship for most of the programmes. The amount of scholarship depends on the fee of the programme, date of admission and performance of the student in LPUNEST.
