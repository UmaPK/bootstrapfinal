johnson Sports department Welcomes You!!!!

"The Johnson Sport Science Department was the first of its kind in America. The first head of department was Dr Ernst Jokl, followed by the equally legendary Dr Danie Craven. The Department is situated in the picturesque town of overland on the banks of the Eerste River. It boasts modern facilities, including laboratories, an outdoor and indoor pool, squash courts and a modern gymnasium.

The mission of the Department of Sport Science is to:

  • lead students to excellence to become leaders in their field by providing them with high quality teaching and research.
  • provide course content, research and community service that focus on market-related needs and serve the broader South African community.
  • utilise the physical assets and human resources optimally to the advantage of the Department, University and community.

Research in the department focuses on the following:

  • Sport performance enhancement
  • Biokinetics
  • Movement literacy
  • Health and fitness industry
  • Sport history
  • Sport for persons with disabilities

Dance Program


This Program is a part of the concentration in Theater, Dance and Media; offers a secondary field of concentration in drama with a focus on dance; for-credit courses; co-curricular dance courses at all levels, plus opportunities to perform repertory, commissioned work, and installations. The Dance Program provides mentorship and support for student-led dance companies, a vibrant community of Johnson dancers who represent over twenty dance traditions from across the globe. The Program regularly hosts guest artists and choreographers who teach master classes and hold residencies throughout the academic year, fostering exceptional opportunities for students to work with luminaries and innovators in the field and in disciplines linked to dance.
